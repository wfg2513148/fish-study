一、单项选择 共 15 题，每题 2 分，共 30 分

  1. This is my friend. ______ name is Tom.
    A. HeB. HisC. SheD. Her
  2. There ______ a book and two pens on the desk.
    A. amB. isC. areD. be
  3. My sister often ______ her homework after dinner.
    A. doB. doesC. doingD. did
  4. ______ do you go to school? By bike.
    A. WhatB. WhenC. HowD. Where
  5. Listen! The students ______ English songs.
    A. singB. singsC. are singingD. sang
  6. We have math ______ Monday morning.
    A. inB. onC. atD. for
  7. I like pandas ______ they are cute.
    A. soB. butC. becauseD. or
  8. The bank is ______ the library and the supermarket.
    A. betweenB. underC. behindD. from
  9. Please ______ quiet in the reading room.
    A. isB. areC. beD. being
  10. I can play ______ chess, but I can't play ______ guitar.
    A. the; theB. /; theC. the; /D. /; /
  11. It takes me twenty minutes ______ to school.
    A. getB. getsC. to getD. getting
  12. My mother is strict ______ me, but she is kind.
    A. withB. forC. aboutD. of
  13. There are ______ people in the park on weekends.
    A. muchB. manyC. anyD. little
  14. What does your father look like? He ______ tall and thin.
    A. isB. hasC. doesD. likes
  15. Would you like some noodles? ______.
    A. Yes, pleaseB. You're welcomeC. Good ideaD. That's all right

二、完形填空 共 10 题，每题 2 分，共 20 分

It is Saturday morning. Lucy and her brother Jack go to the old people's home with their classmates. They want to do something useful. Lucy can sing, so she sings two songs. Jack is good at drawing, and he draws a picture for an old man. Their classmates clean the rooms and read newspapers to the old people. At noon, everyone feels tired but happy.

  1. Lucy and Jack go there on ______.
  2. They go there with their ______.
  3. Lucy can ______.
  4. Jack draws a picture for ______.
  5. The students feel tired but ______.

三、阅读理解 共 10 题，每题 3 分，共 30 分

Passage A

Mike lives near a small zoo. On Sundays, he often goes there with his cousin. His favorite animal is the elephant because it is smart and friendly. But he doesn't like tigers. He thinks they are scary. The zoo opens at 9:00 a.m. and closes at 5:00 p.m.

Passage B

Anna is a middle school student. She has many rules at home. She must get up at 6:30. She has to make her bed before breakfast. After school, she must finish homework first. She can't watch TV on school nights. Anna thinks rules are useful, but sometimes she wants more free time.

  1. Where does Mike live?
  2. Why does Mike like elephants?
  3. How long is the zoo open every day?
  4. What must Anna do before breakfast?
  5. Can Anna watch TV on school nights?
  6. Where is the library?
  7. What is across from the bank?
  8. How can you get from the school to the park?
  9. Which place is south of the school?
  10. Which place is east of the school?

四、词汇与语法 共 10 题，每题 2 分，共 20 分

  1. 用所给词适当形式填空：Look! They ______ (play) basketball.
  2. 用所给词适当形式填空：My brother ______ (brush) his teeth at 7:00.
  3. 句型转换：She can swim. 改为一般疑问句。
  4. 句型转换：The school is behind the park. 对画线部分提问。
  5. 翻译短语：遵守规则
